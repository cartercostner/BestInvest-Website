Frambulance

Streamlining the emergency rescue response

April 15, 2014 Frambulance

Company Description

Frambulance was created us, George Gervin and Artis Gilmore, in 2014 after a freak accident left us devistated. When we called 911 to get emergency responders to our location, the ambulance was five minutes earlier on arrival then the firetruck. We knew there was a better solution. We knew there was a place in the market for an emergency response vehicle that could satisfy the needs of an ambulance and a firetruck. We began working on our business plan and now are looking for solidworks drafters to help design a prototype Frambulance for investors.
